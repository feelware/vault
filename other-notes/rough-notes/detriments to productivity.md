## laziness/task paralysis

lack of strength to do certain tasks. often, even the idea of such tasks being difficult results in **task paralysis** and **brain fog**

## lack of sleep (!!!)

leads to irritability. leads to falling asleep during my shift. exacerbates other vulnerabilities

## music

hijacks my attention until I get bored of it, which can take from a single song to an entire album. I can hardly focus on a (mentally demanding) task with music

## lack of attention to current task

leads to forgetting the current goal, which leads to autopilot: a state of extreme vulnerability to distractions (such as using the phone or doing an **internal monologue**) in search of paths of least resistance. this shows a lot while taking a shower and doing other chores

## overthinking

obsession over minimal annoyances

## not tracking time left

not having panorama leads to running out of time in the long run
